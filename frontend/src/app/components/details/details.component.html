Day 1 - Rural Plains and Cura Monastery

Your first ride of the trip will take you through the heart of the island, what we call here the ‘rural plains’. The rolling terrain through charming villages, fields, and vineyards offers a great introduction to the island. The Cappuccinos will loop back north from here and the Espressos will ride south to Cura Monastery, located on top of a mountain.
